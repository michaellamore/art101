Outline an everyday task

Top-secret recipe to make crazy good orange chicken at home

  1. Make the sauce
    1. Mix 3/4 cup of granulated sugar and 3/4 cup of white vinegar onto a heated saucepan.
    2. Add 1/3 cup + 1 tbsp of soy sauce and 1/4 cup of water onto the mixture.
    3. Add 1 orange's zest and juice, then mix.
    4. Once the sauce starts to boil over high heat, reduce the heat to medium.
    5. Let it simmer for 8-10 minutes.
    6. Separately, mix 1 tbsp of cornstarch with 1 tbsp of water, then whisk it into the saucepan.
    7. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens.
    8. Add some grated cloves of garlic and ginger, then let it steep.
  2. Make the chicken
    1. Combine 1 cup of cornstarch with 1/2 cup of flour onto a bowl.
    2. Whisk two eggs together in a separate bowl.
    3. Cut 1 + 1/2 lbs of chicken thighs into the bite-sized pieces, and lightly season with salt.
    4. Mix the chicken pieces onto the cornstarch and flour, then onto the eggs, and back onto the cornstarch mixture.
    5. Heat some oil until it is boiling hot, and fry the chicken pieces for 3-5 minutes until they are golden brown.
  3. Combine the chicken with the sauce!
